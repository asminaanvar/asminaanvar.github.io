Project Details

➽ Overview:
AirPure Innovations is a startup tackling India’s air quality crisis, where 14 of the 20 most polluted global cities are located. The project explored whether sustained demand exists for air purifiers and how data-driven insights can guide product development.

➽ Problem:
As part of project, I took on the role of Data Analyst (Peter Pandey) to support AirPure Innovations in making data-driven decisions before launching their air purifier

So, I analyzed the Severity Mapping Identifying cities with persistent/worsening AQI trends. Health Impact Correlation,vehicle Impact Correlation,population relation Demand Triggers Understanding how spikes in pollution influence consumer adoption ,google Trends and market Research on how the Airpurifier market to be explored .

➽ Process Flow:
1. Data Collection & Validation (Python, Pandas): Ensured data consistency and accuracy.
2.Data Cleaning & Preprocessing: Removed invalid entries, handled null values.
3.Exploratory & Business Analysis (Power BI): Built dashboards with KPIs, trendlines, and comparative insights across AQI, health, vehicle, and population data (2022–2025)

➽ Conclusion:
The analysis confirms that PM2.5 and PM10 are the most critical pollutants, with cities like Delhi, Ghaziabad, and Lucknow showing the highest sustained demand for air purifiers. By aligning R&D with localized pollution patterns and integrating consumer-valued features such as real-time AQI monitoring and smart connectivity, AirPure Innovations can position itself as a market leader while addressing India’s urgent air quality challenge.
